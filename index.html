About CreaTech

Putting the Pieces Together

CreaTech Products and Consulting is a sole proprietorship owned and operated by Brad Smalley. Brad is an Information Technology (IT) professional, technology enthusiast, and non-apologetic nerd. Brad has a Bachelor of Science Degree in Information Technology with a Software emphasis from Western Governors University. Brad also holds a number of IT related certifications including Oracle Certified Java Programmer, Microsoft Technology Associate (Network, Progamming, Security, and Database Fundamentals), CompTIA Project+, and Certification Partners' Certified Internet Webmaster (CIW) Javascript, Perl, and Database Specialist. During his years as an IT professional, Brad has been successful with a number of programming languages including COBOL, Easytrieve, Java, Groovy, C#, and Visual Basic and, a variety of databases including IBM DB2, Microsoft SQL Server, and even IBM Information Management System (IMS/DB). Truly a nerd to the core, Brad enjoys tinkering with programmable microcontrollers like Arduino, Raspberry Pi, ESP8266, and more. Brad's experience from mainframe to microcontroller gives him a wide angle view of Information Technology that ensures he will keep "Putting the Pieces Together."
